Your privacy is our priority
All consultations are confidential and handled by licensed clinicians.
  • Services: General gynecology, fertility counseling, prenatal & postnatal care, menstrual & PCOS care, infections & STI screening, family planning, mental health support.
  • Reproductive Health Counseling: Clear, judgment-free guidance to understand reproductive health, risks, and safe practices — provided strictly within UAE regulations.
  • Pregnancy Options Counseling: Confidential discussions to help you understand what care is legally available. In the UAE, termination of pregnancy is only considered in very limited medical situations such as risk to the mother’s life or severe fetal abnormalities confirmed by specialists.
  • Emergency: If you are in immediate danger or experiencing heavy bleeding, go to the nearest emergency department or call local emergency services.

About Us

Our Commitment to Safe, Lawful, Patient‑Centered Care

Serene Women’s Health is a Dubai‑based clinic providing women’s wellness and reproductive health counseling. We operate strictly within UAE laws, offering education, prevention, prenatal and gynecologic care, and referrals to licensed services when appropriate.

  • Privacy: Discreet, confidential consultations. Your information is protected.
  • Access: In‑person and telehealth options with Arabic and English support.
  • Quality: Evidence‑based protocols, continuous training, and peer review.
